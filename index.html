HOW IT WORKS

Your complete journey
from signup to trade

Every step is designed to be intuitive, secure, and rewarding. Here's how the entire experience flows.

PHASE 01

Getting Started

Sign Up Instantly

One tap with Apple or Google. No forms, no verification emails. Your identity is verified through OAuth from the very first second.

Choose Your Language

Pick from 22 languages including Arabic, Kurdish, Persian with full RTL support. The entire app adapts to your language and culture.

Set Your Location

Enable location services to discover items near you. Adjust your search radius from 1km to 500km. Your precise location is never shared with other users.

PHASE 02

Discovering & Connecting

Browse & Filter

Explore the marketplace with powerful filters: category, price range, location radius, condition, and more. View items on an interactive map or in a scrollable feed.

Save & Get Alerts

Save your search criteria as a Search Alert. When a new listing matches, you get an instant push notification — so you never miss a deal.

Chat with Encryption

Start a conversation with end-to-end encryption. Send text, images, voice messages, share your location, and negotiate directly — all secured.

PHASE 03

Trading & Earning

Make & Receive Offers

Use the structured offer system to propose, counter-offer, accept, or decline. Every offer is tracked with timestamps and full transaction history.

Complete the Trade

Once agreed, complete the trade. A detailed transaction receipt is generated with all the details — item, price, parties involved, timestamp, and status.

Review & Earn Badges

Leave a review for your trading partner. Earn badges for completed trades, daily activity streaks, and community engagement. Build your reputation over time.

PHASE 04

Growing Your Presence

Track Everything

Your profile shows your complete history: listings, reviews, badges, reputation score, and wallet balance. Full transparency, full control.
