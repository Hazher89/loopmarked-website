Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first listing?

▼

Open Loop Marked, tap the "+" button in the bottom navigation bar, add photos of your item, fill in the title, description, price (in Lumo), and select a category. Tap "Create Listing" and your item will be submitted for review.

What is Lumo and how do I earn it?

▼

Lumo is the internal currency of Loop Marked. You can earn Lumo by: claiming your daily login bonus, watching reward videos, inviting friends, completing trades, and achieving badges. You can also purchase Lumo directly through in-app purchases.

Is my data safe on Loop Marked?

▼

Yes. Loop Marked uses end-to-end encryption (AES-256 + RSA) for all chat messages, images, and voice notes. Your precise location is never shared with other users.

How do I delete my account?

▼

Go to Settings → Delete Account, or navigate to Settings → Privacy & Security → scroll to the bottom → Delete Account. You will be guided through a confirmation dialog before your account and all data are permanently removed.

How do I report a suspicious user or listing?

▼

On a listing, tap the menu (⋯) icon and select "Report." In a chat, tap the user's profile and choose "Report User." You can also contact us at support@loopmarked.com.

What languages does Loop Marked support?

▼

Loop Marked is available in 22 languages including English, Norwegian, Arabic, Kurdish (Sorani & Kurmanji), Persian, Turkish, Russian, Ukrainian, Spanish, French, German, and more — with full RTL support.
