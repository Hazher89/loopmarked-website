Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Loop Marked. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services.

Information We Collect

To provide you with our marketplace services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address or authentication tokens from Apple/Google.
  • Profile Information: Your chosen display name, profile picture, and bio.
  • Location Data: To show you local listings, we collect your precise or approximate location when you use the app.
  • User Content: Information about items you list for sale, photos you upload, messages you send via our chat system, and reviews you leave for other users.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our app, including device identifiers and crash logs to improve app performance.
  • Transaction Data: Information related to your Lumo balance and in-app purchases. (Payment processing is handled securely by Apple; we do not store your credit card details).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our app and services.
  • Process transactions and manage your Lumo economy balance.
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers.
  • Verify accounts and enhance the safety and security of our platform.
  • Send support messages, updates, and security alerts.
  • Provide personalized, local content and marketplace listings.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With other users: Your profile information, listings, and reviews are visible to other users on the platform.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services (like Supabase for database hosting) to help operate our app. These providers have access to your information only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can delete your account and associated data at any time directly within the app settings or by contacting our support team via chat.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage your privacy choices within the app settings, including visibility toggles for your wallet balance and other specific information.

Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support chat in the app or at support@loopmarked.com.
